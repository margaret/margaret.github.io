Berkeley Math Tournament

"The Berkeley Math Tournament Group hosts and organizes mathematical contests for middle and high school students. In organizing these tournaments, the team strives to encourage mathematical exploration and understanding by introducing concepts not covered in the typical pre-college curricula to students with high mathematical aptitude and interest. Each contest emphasizes collaboration between team members, while still allowing individuals to prove their own ability."

I got lured in as a day volunteer one semester by promises of free food and milk tea, and I enjoyed it so much I stayed. It's been challenging and fulfilling organizing such a large event. I'm currently on staff as the head of logistics.
